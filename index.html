About Streamers Academy

Our mission is to inspire and empower you to launch or grow your own Twitch streaming channel. As Streamer’s Academy evolves and expands, we will provide our streamers with a growing collection of tools and direction to cultivate their own viral channels and unique brands. With progress, our dream is to very soon maintain a supportive streamer community that comprises a network of both peers and professional content creators.

Anyone with a passion for gaming and a desire to stream has the power to found their own original channel and achieve greatness, but the strategic aspects of an effective stream present real challenges. Streamers Academy, is creating a collaborative opportunity for new and novice streamers to be matched with more experienced and successful content creators for coaching and mentorship. In our Alpha Phase, we are offering a small and selective inaugural class the opportunity to grow themselves and their streams with a single coach. While this is a first come first serve opportunity, prospective mentees must first apply and qualify with a two-round, informal interview process - one round with us, and one with their coach. Discord is our preferred channel of communication to hold these “interviews.” We regret that we cannot take all comers in this early Alpha phase. If you have applied and were not admitted to this inaugural class, rest assured that there will be more opportunities in our Beta and Launch phases to come. Those who apply or sign up for our Alpha and are not accepted will be given the opportunity to participate early in our Beta. We welcome and encourage anyone with a true passion for gaming and streaming to apply!

While it holds true that the most important ingredients to a wildly successful channel are the ambition and creativity of its host, even the most popular streamers of today leverage friends and mentors to maintain and grow their streams and themselves. With this understanding and our own profound passion for helping fellow streamers achieve success, we at Streamers’ Academy invite you to join our ranks. Leverage us as your friends and mentors. Start your own Twitch channel or grow the one you already have. Find your audience. Feed your passion. Stream it to the world.
