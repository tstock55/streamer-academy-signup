Coaching for Streamers

Why Stream Coaching?

Getting better at streaming is the number one reason to work with a coach. Whether your objective is improving your content, viewership or sponsorships, your coach will help you get there. Meeting an experienced streamer, expanding your personal network, and finding additional support and motivation are just fringe benefits to the number one goal: Get better at streaming.

How Does Coaching Work?

Now, in our Alpha, we work with a single twitch-partnered coach, so mentee space is limited. Prospective mentees are given opportunities to be coached on a first come first serve basis. After a quick consultation with us at Streamers Academy, mentees may then schedule coaching in hour-long blocks.
